SMS Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 12, 2026

Who We Are

This SMS messaging service is operated by the Cork family ("we," "us," "our") located in Arizona. Our family assistant, Friday, sends text messages on our behalf to contacts who have provided their phone number directly to us.

What Information We Collect

When you opt in to receive SMS messages from us, we collect:

  • Your phone number
  • Your name (if provided)
  • Message content you send to us

How We Use Your Information

We use your phone number and related information solely to:

  • Send personal notifications, reminders, and replies
  • Respond to your incoming messages
  • Send appointment reminders and follow-ups
  • Share family-related updates with contacts who have requested them

Information Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. Your phone number and message content are only accessible to members of the Cork family and our automated assistant system.

We use Twilio as our messaging platform provider. Twilio processes messages in accordance with their own Privacy Policy.

Data Retention

We retain your phone number and message history for as long as you remain opted in to receive messages. If you opt out, we will remove your information within 30 days.

Opt-Out

You can stop receiving SMS messages at any time by replying STOP to any message. You will receive a confirmation and no further messages will be sent. Reply START to opt back in.

Help

Reply HELP to any message for assistance, or contact us at joshua.cork@gmail.com.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
